## 🏫 About PSPH

Prasanna School of Public Health (PSPH), **Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE)**, works to improve population health through:

- 📚 Public health education & capacity building  
- 🔬 Evidence-based research  
- 📊 Policy-relevant analytics  
- 🤝 Community & health systems engagement  

---

## ✨ What you’ll find here

### 💬 Interactive demos & tools
- 🧠 **Chatbot / Q&A** for public health information discovery  
- 📄 **Document understanding** (summaries, extraction, insights from reports)  
- 📈 **Data analysis helpers** (survey insights, epi basics, visualization)  
- 🗣️ **Health communication** prototypes (plain-language explanations)  

> **Note:** Repositories are for education/research and may evolve over time.
